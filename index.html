Instagram Follower Analyzer

Process accounts securely, locally, and completely inside your client web browser.

📋 How to Download Your Instagram Data

🔒 Your Privacy Matters: This analyzer runs 100% locally in your browser. Your data is never uploaded to any server.

Step 1: Request Your Information

  • Open Instagram and go to Settings and Activity.
  • Open Accounts Center.
  • Navigate to Your information and permissions.
  • Click Export your information.

Step 2: Configure Export

  • Select Export to device.
  • Choose Customise information.
  • Select only Followers and following.
  • Set format to JSON.
  • Click Start export.

Step 3: Extract Required Files

  • Download the ZIP file from Instagram.
  • Extract the ZIP archive.
  • Locate these files:
    • followers_1.json
    • following.json
  • Upload both files into the analyzer.
